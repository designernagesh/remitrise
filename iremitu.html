iRemitU

Welcome to iRemitU – the future of international remittance by RemitRise. iRemitU is our advanced solution designed to simplify and secure cross-border transactions for both individuals and businesses. Accessible via web and mobile applications (Android, iOS, and Huawei), iRemitU offers a comprehensive suite of features tailored to meet diverse remittance needs

Our platform supports various transaction types, including P2P, P2B, B2P, and B2B, with options for bank transfers, cash pick-ups, and cash delivery. iRemitU boasts robust portals for customers, administrators, and agents, ensuring seamless user experiences and efficient management.

Key features include a user-friendly customer portal, an admin portal with compliance and treasury management, and a secure, scalable microservices architecture. Integrated with leading payment gateways and notification systems, iRemitU ensures real-time, reliable transactions.

Join us in revolutionizing the international remittance landscape with iRemitU – where technology meets trust.

The iRemitU suite from RemitRise enhances the remittance process through its comprehensive Customer, Admin, and Agent Portals, along with versatile mobile apps. The Customer Portal provides users with an intuitive interface for managing transactions, tracking payments, and accessing support. The Admin Portal empowers administrators with tools for overseeing operations, monitoring transactions, and generating reports. The Agent Portal allows agents to facilitate transactions, manage customer inquiries, and provide personalized services. Mobile app options, including web-based and hybrid apps, ensure seamless and efficient user experiences across Android, iOS, and Huawei platforms. These portals and mobile apps collectively streamline operations, improve customer service, and enhance overall efficiency by providing real-time access, robust security, and customizable solutions to meet diverse business needs.
